Why is this website "best viewed" on Linux?

Short answer: Because everything looks better on Linux.

Long answer:
If you are on a Windows computer and you have not changed the default system zoom, you are likely seeing the website at 125% size.

I have found this to look somewhat awkward because it worsens the site graphics quality, and makes scroll-bars show up where I don't intend them to.

If you want, you can either switch to Linux (based) or zoom out to 80% to offset Windows's default 125% zoom.
