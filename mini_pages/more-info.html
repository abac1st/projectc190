FAQ
How do I make my own website?

I suggest you start with the HTML and CSS guides on W3Schools and just start making random shit. That's how most people start. Have fun, and feel free to let me know about your work in my guestbook.
Why is this website "best viewed" on Linux?

If you are on Windows and you have not changed the default system zoom, you are likely seeing the website at 125% size. I have found this to look awkward because it worsens the site graphics quality, and makes scroll-bars show up where I don't intend them to. If you want, you can either be cool and switch to Linux, or zoom out to 80% to offset Windows's default 125% zoom.
What did you use to make the graphics on this website?

A combination of MS Paint, KolourPaint, and Scratch.
